Snow Profile Editor

Instructions

Use your mouse to drag the handle (black square) to indicate the depth and hardness of each layer. The (depth, hardness) values will appear to the right of the chart when you click on the handle.

Click an Insert button to insert another layer at that point.

Click the Edit button in a layer to edit the description of a layer or delete the layer.

Click the Preview button to see how the diagram will look.
